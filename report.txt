Common Password Attacks:
1. Brute Force Attack:
   Attackers try every possible combination.

2. Dictionary Attack:
   Attackers use common words and passwords from databases.

Importance of Password Length:
Long passwords are harder to crack and increase security significantly.

What is Multi-Factor Authentication?
MFA adds an extra verification step such as OTP or fingerprint.

How Password Managers Help:
Password managers store and generate secure passwords safely.

Best Practices Learned:
- Use long passwords
- Include symbols and numbers
- Avoid common words
- Use unique passwords for every account
- Enable MFA whenever possible

Conclusion:
Strong passwords significantly improve account security and protect against cyber attacks.
